When you think about the figures in a scientific paper, you probably think of dreary graphs or tissue sections, maybe an anatomical diagram or two. For the most part, scientific publishing aims to be as efficient as possible. It refuses to accept figures that serve no important explanatory purpose, and strives to be elegant and streamlined. For the most part. But every now and then... a complete and utter meme slips through the cracks.
