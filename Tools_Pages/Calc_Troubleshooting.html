Using the calculator:

When you open the page it should look like this. You only need to look at the top part. Hide the nasty and confusing bottom screen by dragging the top screen down, as so. Fill out the form and hit submit at the bottom.

Checking your results:

After hitting submit, the terminal should give you a number, in this case it's -2. Check this number against the table below the terminal. We can see that -2 is a very bad score, meaning the article I'm checking is very poor quality!
